What's on next?

A Helsinki-based collective bridging diverse art and music scenes. Rooted in DJ culture and event-making, we create welcoming spaces where self-expression, collaboration, and creativity thrive. We believe in the power of community to bring people together — on the dance floor and beyond.

Subterra is a non-profit organization built on creativity, friendship, and mutual respect.
